# Floor plan – full clarity

The heatmap uses the ground floor plan from the **project root** `public/` folder:

- **`/GroundFloor.svg`** (recommended) – Vector. Stays sharp at any zoom. Convert your PDF to SVG (e.g. [CloudConvert](https://cloudconvert.com/pdf-to-svg), Inkscape, or Adobe Acrobat export) and save as `public/GroundFloor.svg`.
- **`/GroundFloor.png`** – Raster fallback. For best clarity use a high‑resolution export (e.g. 2× or 3×) from the PDF.

The map **fits to the container** on load and **won’t shrink** when you zoom out (minimum zoom = fit). Use **Fit** to reset view; **+ / −** or scroll to zoom; drag to pan.
